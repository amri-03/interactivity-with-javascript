Digital Photography

Digital photography captures diverse subjects from intimate macro flowers to vast night skies. Whether shooting urban streets, serene forest scenes, or dramatic silhouettes, understanding your camera's exposure triangle—aperture, shutter speed, and ISO—gives you creative control in any situation.

This collection showcases various photography styles including nature photography with water reflections, detailed flower close-ups using macro techniques, atmospheric forest scenes, dynamic urban life, and challenging night sky photography. Each style requires different approaches: street photography demands quick reflexes and good timing, while nature photography benefits from patience and golden hour lighting. Post-processing skills help enhance the natural beauty captured in scenes like silhouettes against dramatic skies or the peaceful reflections found in still water.
